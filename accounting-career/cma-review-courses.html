#4: Surgent CMA Review Course


OVERALL RATING:

Surgent CMA Review Course Overview


Surgent CMA Review Course Includes:

2,500+ Multiple Choice - 35 Essay Problems - Simulated Exam Mode - Textbooks


The Good

  • Adaptive Learning Technology - Surgent CMA Review is not a linear CMA review course. It utilizes adaptive learning technology to help you identify what topics you know (and which you don't). This means that instead of reading your textbook cover to cover Surgent will assign you topics to study based on your practice exam results.

  • Multiple Choice - Surgent CMA Review includes 2,500+ multiple choice questions

The Bad

  • Lack Video Lectures - If you are someone who needs an instructor to learn then Surgent CMA may not be for you as their teaching method is very unique.

  • Reference Books Lacking Depth - The Surgent CMA Review textbook is more of a dictionary or reference book, it doesn't provide in-depth examples of questions or provide in-depth explanations for concepts.
