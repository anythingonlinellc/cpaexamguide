#3: Surgent EA Review Course

OVERALL RATING:

Surgent EA Review Course Overview




Surgent EA Review Course Includes:

1,800+ Multiple Choice - Adaptive Learning - Pass Guarantee


The Good

  • Adaptive Learning Technology - Surgent EA Review utilizes a smart learning algorithm that automatically identifies what you need to learn and what you already understand based on how you answer their multiple choice questions. This allows you to save time by not doubling up on your study time over concepts you already understand.

  • Multiple Choice - Surgent EA Review includes 1,800+ multiple choice questions

  • Multiple Choice Analytics - Built into their adaptive learning technology and similar to Gleim EA, Surgent EA Review offers in-depth multiple choice analytics so you can easily track your results.

  • Smart Textbook - Surgent EA Review offers a feature that connects each multiple choice question directly to the textbook so that you can easily research why a question is right or wrong. The Bad

  • Lack Video Lectures - If you are someone who needs an instructor to learn then Surgent EA may not be for you.

  • Reference Books Lacking Depth - The Surgent EA Review textbook is more of a dictionary or reference book, it doesn't provide in-depth examples of questions or provide in-depth explanations for concepts.
