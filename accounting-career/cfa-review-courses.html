#1: Wiley CFA® Program Review Course


OVERALL RATING:


Wiley CFA® Program Review Course Overview

Wiley CFA® Program Review is a top tier CFA® Prep Course for serious CFA® exam candidates who are serious about passing the CFA® exam on their first try.

Wiley is an industry leader in the CPA test prep space and has taken the same proven study technology and applied it to their CFA® exam review course.


Wiley CFA® Program Review Course Includes:

4,000+ Multiple Choice - 3 Mock Exams - Self-Study or Virtual Classes - Bite Sized Video Lectures


The Good

  • Bite-Sized Lectures - Wiley CFA® Program Review Course (just like it's Wiley CPAexcel counterpart) utilizes bite-sized video lectures to help you quickly learn the material and most importantly keep you focused on studying instead of day dreaming.

  • Professional Online Mentoring - Have a question about anything CFA® exam related? Wiley CFA® Program has a support team who will provide you with a response within 24-48 hours.

  • 11th Hour Final Review - Worried about exam day? Wiley CFA® Program provides you with an 11th hour final review product that includes 40 hours of final review lectures and a Mock Exam Seminar.

  • Performance Metrics - Track your results and monitor your weaknesses within the Wiley CFA® Program dashboard so you know where to spend your time studying.

The Bad

  • Pricing - Wiley CFA® Program Course offers three pricing tiers that range from $595 to $1,295 per level of the CFA® exam (before discounts). This is a bit more expensive compared to other courses in the industry, BUT if you are serious about passing the CFA® exam getting access to Wiley CFA® Program's resources will be well worth the investment (in my opinion) compare to the less expensive courses that can't compete.
