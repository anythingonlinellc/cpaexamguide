Becker CPA Review Course Pros & Cons [2019]

Just because a course is expensive doesn't mean it's the best.

Unlimited Access

After years of limiting software access, Becker CPA review FINALLY gives CPA candidates unlimited access until they pass. This is important because if life gets in the way of the exam, you don't want to scramble to find a new course in the middle of studying.

NOTE: This only applies to the Premium and Pro packages. Advantage only offers 24 months access.

Video Lectures + Textbooks + Mobile App

Written materials are top notch with plenty of examples and mnemonics and the lectures are fairly engaging and alibeit super corny at times will keep you focused. The lectures do run a bit longer than most other CPA review courses. Everything can also be accessed and used on their mobile app for complete freedom as you study.

Multiple Choice

Multiple choice explanations are excellent and will help you truly understand why a question is right or wrong.

Digital & Printed Flashcards

Some of the best Flashcards in the industry with detailed definitions of the most tested material.

Success Coach & Tutoring

Depending on the Becker package you choose you may have access to a CPA exam success coach and/or 5 hours of 1-on-1 tutoring.

This isn't quite at the same level as a CPA exam mentor, but it certainly helps to have additional help.

LiveOnline Classroom

With the Premium and Pro Packages, you now have access to sign up to join online lecture series instead of commuting to a live location. This is a huge time saver and you will receive the same structure of being in a live classroom with the ability to watch replays. I still do not recommend the live classroom option as it is just too inefficient for most CPA candidates and quality of instructors is hit or miss.

Price

Becker CPA Review is still the most premiumly priced course in the industry, unless they are offering a discount. If you are paying out of pocket be sure to compare Becker to other CPA review courses to ensure it's the right fit for you or use their payment plans to spread your expense. I do like the Advantage package they offer which starts at $2,399 for budget-minded CPA candidates, just don't expect unlimited access to the software or access to their final review or success coaches.

Standard Customer Service

According to their website, they have very limited hours on the weekend. Expect 2 full business days for a response per their website. The majority of students study during the weekend and need a quick response which in my opinion shouldn't be more than 24 hours. When sending in an email I would recommend you batch your questions together to speed up the process.
