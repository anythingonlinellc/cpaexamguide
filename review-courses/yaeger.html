How Easy Is Yaeger CPA Review To Use?

When you invest in a CPA Review Course, you want a course that will help you easily navigate the content and help you truly understand the material.

When you login to Yaeger for the first time you will want to select the section you plan to start studying for first.

Next you explain exactly how you learn best or would prefer to study. Yaeger will then tailor your experience based on your preference.  

Once inside the course each section is broken down into Chapters that are labeled by AICPA Blueprint.

This is important because you only need to know what is covered in the AICPA blueprint for the2020 CPA exam and beyond.

Each chapter contains a lecture, sample questions, a digital copy of the textbook, and the ability to bookmark and take notes.

Yaeger CPA Review offers what's called ADAPTAPASS technology to track your results.

They use proprietary technology to identify how prepared you are for the CPA exam based on your progress through their course.

They give you an "ADAPTASCORE" that you can monitor on your dashboard.

Use can use this score to identify the areas you need to focus your time on studying.

Yaeger CPA Review Multiple Choice & Simulations


How Do Yaeger CPA Review's CPA Exam Questions Compare?

As part of Yaeger CPA Review's Adaptapass system they give you multiple ways to quiz yourself.

You have the option to test your knowledge by AIPCA Blueprint Content Groups, by a custom quiz generator, by flashcards and by Exam Simulator.

By reviewing your ADAPTASCORE you are easily able to test yourself on those content groups.

I found that the multiple choice dashboard was easy to use and that the questions were similar to what you can expect to see on the CPA exam.

Yaeger CPA Review also offers in depth explanation for each question you answer of it's 6,000+ questions.

It would be nice if the explanations were tied better to your text just in case you needed to do additional research but since each question is categorized by content group, it wouldn't take much time to find the relevant section to review from the text.

I also wish there was an option to automatically see the answer instead of waiting until the end.

But after each quiz it's easy to see why you got the question is right or wrong, so not a deal breaker.

Simulations are also cutting edge and based on all the2020 changes to the exam and score weighting you can expect to see on the real exam.

After speaking with Phil Yaeger himself, he is seeing a shift in focusing on testing of your knowledge away from multiple choice and putting all the focus on simulations.

So regardless of the course you pick, you should recognize this shift and ensure your course has up to date simulations for you to prepare with.

Yaeger CPA Review Video Lectures

What Can You Expect From Yaeger CPA Review's Video Lectures?

Yaeger CPA Review utilizes multiple accounting professors and CPA exam experts in addition to Dr. Yaeger to create the video lectures that will walk you through the textbook.

The feeling I get from these lectures are that the instruction you get will be identical to being in your college class.

They do keep the lecture times relatively short (between 8 -60 minutes) which is good because I didn't necessarily find any one professor to be a stand out entertainer that you might find in a course like UWorld Roger CPA Review.

However, if you can get over the dry nature of the presentation, they get right to the point and will teach you the material which is what really matters.
