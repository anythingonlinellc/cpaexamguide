Key Features Uworld CPA Review Course Has to Offer

Software Platform Interface + Mobile App

Uworld CPA has two ways you can consume the learning material -- the desktop software platform and through the mobile app.

Let’s talk about the desktop view first. The interface is clean and easy to navigate with a sidebar that you click on where you can jump to any of the material within the section you’re studying for OR switch over to a different section if you’re one of the ambitious types studying for multiple sections at once. And once you click your option the main content shows up in the middle of the screen.

It’s all very intuitive, including the little “hamburger” menu that pops open the sidebar.


One of my pet peeves is when you have a mobile app that uses a totally different logic or foundational user experience than the desktop or web experience.

You do NOT have to worry about that here Roger CPA Review. The mobile app gives you a consistent experience optimized for efficient studying on your phone.

Qbank

Uworld Roger CPA review has a wide variety of questions and simulations (over 6,000!) to help you learn the material you need to pass the CPA exam.

It also provides the option for you to create mini tests based on a pre-set list of topics related to the section you’re studying.

Each question comes with a detailed explanation of the correct answer, and you have the option to go back to past tests you created. That comes in handy if it was a particularly tricky one and you want to go back to it later after you’ve refreshed on the areas you didn’t answer correctly.

There is also a performance tab with basic analytics built into the test bank to help you track at a high level where you need to be focusing your time.

These analytics aren't quite as "nitty-gritty" as other CPA review courses out there so I do hope that they add more granular deep diving capabilities in the future.



SmartPath Technology

After helping thousands of people pass the CPA Exam, it shouldn't be a surprise that Uworld CPA has a massive amount of data on what successful future CPAs look like.

With SmartPath technology, they monitor your progress and compare against how previous students who passed the exam performed in order to guide your study path and set targets for a more efficient path to becoming a CPA.

Sounds pretty smart to me. Yay for data-driven technological advances!


Mock Exams

Back by popular demand, UWorld Roger CPA Review added a Mock Exam feature into their platform.

You can now create unlimited practice/mock exams within the QBank so that you can truly live the Prometric Experience over and over prior to your next exam.

This is crucial for time management and also for testing your understanding of the material.

Flashcard & Digital Notes Overview

Another nice feature Uworld Roger CPA offers is their flash and digital notes system.

They have a canned population of flashcards (“Expert Decks”) which cover hundreds of mnemonic tips and concepts to aid your studying.

These are great for getting familiar with some of the terms but they didn’t stop there.

The flashcards also use something Uworld Roger calls “Space Repetition Technology.” The way it works is the Uworld platform adapts to show you easy content less frequently and harder content more frequently to support improved content retention. (I remember having to do this manually when I was first studying the CPA exam so I’m a big fan of this feature.)

You can also add your own digital notes on each lecture plus you can create your own flashcards (“My Decks) to help you study by quickly transferring content from the course interface to a new flashcard, or you can simply type in what you want to spend more time trying to remember.

Technical Support Overview

Support is one of those things where you really hope you never have to use it.

For Uworld Roger CPA Review, the support model looks pretty simple. When you click the help option in the sidebar, you land on a page with some basic how-tos and frequently asked questions.

This probably covers the bulk of anything you might encounter, but there is also a quick tab on the right side of the page in case you need to send a message to cover something that’s not listed on that page.

Study Hub

UWorld Roger CPA Review has also kept an archieved version of the Study Hub available for you to search. It's not active right now, meaning you can't post new questions, but it's very easy to search for questions you have that have already been asked and answered.

For any questions you have on the material you can contact their support team for assistance from within the course or contact their support email.

Where does Uworld Roger CPA Review Need Improvement?

Earlier I mentioned rating Uworld Roger a 4.8/5.0, and when I think about where the course could use improvement, it comes down to 3 things:

1) More access to dig into topics on the Qbank - while I really like how it works, having more precision on where you want to focus within each of the topics they already give you would be a nice update

2) Ability to post questions in the study help feature - This might be a bit of a nit since support is always available via email or phone, but this addition would help build more community amongst the Roger CPA students plus be more convenient for the student with the question posted

3) Improved planning and mentorship - Many courses I’ve seen fall a little short on the planning and mentorship.
