First of all, thank you so much for visiting my site!

I was sitting in your shoes just a few short years ago and I know EXACTLY what it is like being a first time CPA candidate just starting out in Public Accounting.

This is why it is so important that we stay connected, because you don't need to go on this difficult journey alone!

Obviously you are here because you have a question whether its about a review course, how to study for the CPA exam or about your accounting career, so let's address the best way to get it answered!


Leave Me A Voicemail!

If you are interested in having your question about the CPA exam or Public Accounting answered on my podcast or in a video tutorial, you can leave me a voicemail below! 

I am looking for questions related to CPA study problems you may be having or work related issues at your accounting firm or anything that is going to be relevant to accountants.

Voicemail Guidelines: I want this show to be fun, personal, and helpful to everyone, so please keep your voicemails regarding issue you are having with the CPA exam or Public Accounting to no more than one minute in length and be concise and straight to the point. Obviously I will only accept courteous questions.

That's pretty much it. Just click the "Start Recording" button below and speak clearly into your microphone and then enter your email address so that I can get in touch with you :)

Please note that by submitting a voicemail question, you’re allowing me to share it publicly on the podcast. Click here for more details on the Terms of Use.

As you can imagine I do receive tons of questions, please understand that you may not receive a direct response to your question. I will answer qualified questions as they come, but often times I will be receiving duplicate questions and will have to move on to the next question. Please subscribe to my podcast so that you can tune in and see when your question has been answered! Given the volume of questions that I receive, I won’t be able to email you back with a response.

Also, if your request is time-sensitive, then email me directly. It can take me a week or longer to listen to new questions. Thanks for understanding, and thanks for listening! 

If your question is featured then I will send you a CPA Guide exclusive "Stay Calm & Pass The CPA Exam" t-shirt, that you can proudly wear as you dominate the CPA exam! (sorry I can only ship to the 50 US states...)
