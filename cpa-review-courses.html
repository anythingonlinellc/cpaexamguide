Roger CPA Review
(Rank #1)

OVERALL RATING:

Best Lectures In Industry: Roger Phillipp, CPA is the star of the show when you invest in Roger CPA Review. They are not only entertaining but easy to follow as Roger utilizes acronyms and examples to make the complex accounting topics easy to understand.

SmartPath Technology: Roger CPA Review introduced a new technology in 2018 called the SmartPath. The SmartPath is based on thousands of previous Roger CPA Review students who have passed the CPA exam. Roger CPA Review took the data from those successful CPA candidates (questions answered and missed) and then compares those results to yours. As your exam date approaches, Roger CPA can predict how well prepared you are compared to successful CPA candidates.

Customer Support: Roger CPA Review offers some of the most personalized customer support in the industry. If you invest in their Elite course package, you will receive phone and email access to their accounting experts. Even if you don't use their Elite package you still get access to their customer support that strives to give you a response in under 24 hours, many time within just 2 hours (based on my testing).

Analytics Don't Dig AS Deep As Others: Roger CPA Review has multiple choice analytics and the brand new SmartPath, but when you compare the granular data to Gleim or Wiley CPAexcel it does not get to the same granular level of detail. That being said the SmartPath will guide you to the exact areas you should be focusing your time based on the performance of other students who have passed the CPA exam.

TAKE ME TO ROGER CPA REVIEW

Becker CPA Review
(Rank #2)

OVERALL RATING:

Endorsed By Big Four: Becker CPA Review has partnerships with most CPA firms and is considered by most as the "gold standard" of CPA Review Courses. However, due to the fact that it's subsidized by most CPA firms they charge folks who aren't sponsored by a firm close to double what other courses charge. In my opinion they also don't offer features that are any better than the CPA review courses at half the price, so please do your research if you are paying out of pocket for your course.

Lectures & Multiple Choice: Becker offers 7,000+ multiple choice and 100+ hours of high quality video lectures. They also have some of the better multiple choice analytics in the industry. If you have the money or a firm to pay for Becker then it's likely a good choice for you. However if you prefer to self-study and don't like watching video lectures that go on for 45 minutes to an hour at a time, I would look elsewhere.

Price: For $2,400 the quality of the materials and the study resources provided are basically the same as courses around $400 less expensive that come with access until you pass. Compare Becker CPA Review to Roger CPA Review, Wiley CPAexcel and Gleim CPA Review and you will notice that the quality of the video lectures, multiple choice and analytics are around the same quality.

TAKE ME TO BECKER CPA REVIEW

Yaeger CPA Review
(Rank #3)

OVERALL RATING:

AICPA Blueprint Integration: Yaeger CPA Review has rebuilt their course from the ground up in order to teach you only what is tested on the CPA Review Course. Their textbook, lectures and questions are designed to give you a true understanding of the AICPA blueprint content groups so that you can be fully prepared on exam day.

Pricing: Yaeger CPA Review is a full fledge CPA Review Course that includes video lectures, textbooks and complete multiple choice and simulation test bank all for just over $1,399.

Adapt-A-Pass: Yaeger CPA Review has recently launched a brand new multiple choice analytics system designed to track your results as you progress through their CPA course. Based on your adapt-a-score you can know exactly what topics you are ready to be tested and which areas still need work.

Lecture Are A Bit Dry: Yaeger CPA Review's lectures are between 8-20 minutes long which is good because some of their instructors are a bit dry in their presentation. But what's important is that they break them down into a manageable length so that you can maintain your focus and do a great job of working through the AICPA blueprints.

TAKE ME TO YAEGER CPA REVIEW

Wiley CPAexcel Review
(Rank #4)

OVERALL RATING:

Bite Sized Lessons: Wiley CPAexcel is best known for starting the trend of breaking up each lesson into 30-45 minute chunks. Meaning that each video lecture is between 5-20 minutes long and you can expect a similar amount of time to read the textbook. I like this format because it prevents you from falling asleep because you are constantly rotating between Lectures --> Textbook --> Practice Quizzes.

Multiple Choice Questions & Analytics: Wiley CPAexcel currently offers the most CPA exam questions in the industry (just over 12,000). They also provide you with advanced multiple choice analytics that allow you to slice and dice your progress to help you identify your weaknesses so you know where to spend your time studying.

Virtual Classroom Option: If you are someone who needs structure and wants access to live instruction (without leaving your home) then Wiley CPAexcel's virtual classroom is a great resource for you. The virtual classroom is a live webinar that you can attend (or watch a replay of) and ask questions of the presenter.

Learning Curve (for some): Because Wiley CPAexcel is such a powerful and dynamic CPA review course there is a bit of a learning curve to the software. If you are not computer savvy, expect to spend an hour or two learning how each piece fits together. If you put in the time upfront, I promise that you will be able to make Wiley CPAexcel work for you regardless of your study personality, schedule and budget.

TAKE ME TO WILEY CPAEXCEL

Gleim CPA Review
(Rank #5)

OVERALL RATING:

Multiple Choice: Gleim CPA Review offers 10,000+ multiple choice questions to their students. They also include their new SmartAdapt technology which tracks your results as you study and helps you automate your multiple choice analytics filtering so that you always know exactly what you need to study. They also give you the option (unlike courses like Surgent CPA Review) to follow a linear study plan and go at your own pace which is perfect for self-studiers.

Software Access: Gleim CPA Review offers unlimited access to their software and free electronic updates until you pass when you enroll in their premium Gleim CPA review system. This is important because you want a CPA review course that will stick with you until you have passed so you aren't scrambling to find new study materials in the middle of studying.

Customer Support: Gleim assigns you a personal coach who is capable of helping you along the way during your journey to passing the CPA exam. This is a great way to outsource your questions so you don't spin your wheels while studying.

Video Lectures: Gleim's CPA video lectures cover the information you need to know but the dashboard itself is rather clunky and does not integrate with an electronic textbook. This makes it difficult to study on the go since you will need to bring your textbook with you anywhere you want to study.

TAKE ME TO GLEIM CPA REVIEW

Fast Forward Academy CPA Review
(Rank #6)

OVERALL RATING:

Data-Driven Course: Fast Forward Academy prides themselves on providering CPA candidates with the data they need to identify what they know and what they are missing. You can use their clean dashboard to quickly filter your strengths and weaknesses so you can quickly get to the information you need to study.

Video Lectures and Textbook: Fast Forward Academy CPA review is fully integrated with video lectures and electronic textbook that are all linked to multiple choice questions making it easy to study as you complete practice quizzes.

Customer Support: Fast Forward Academy offers a forum where you can ask questions of the instructors & your peers. While they is a great resource I did find that after browsing multiple threads that some responses took over a week to get a response if at all. I see this as part of the growing pains for a new CPA review course that I do hope get improved over time.

TAKE ME TO FAST FORWARD
ACADEMY CPA REVIEW

MDS CPA Review
(Rank #7)

OVERALL RATING:

Live Classroom Setting: MDS CPA Review is an Ohio based CPA review course and they offer some of the best live classes for CPA test preparation around. Unfortunately the founder Steve Martin has retired from instruction and is only limited in his involvement in the course at this time.

Limited Multiple Choice Analytics: If you invest in the online MDS CPA Review, you will find limited multiple choice analytics in their test bank. To me this is a crucial feature to have in a CPA review course.

TAKE ME TO MDS CPA REVIEW

Ninja CPA Review
(Rank #8)

OVERALL RATING:

Powered By Bisk & Jeff's Test Bank: Ninja CPA Review is masterminded by Jeff Elliott, CPA of Another71. He has combined the video lectures of Bisk and created his own study supplements and test bank to make Ninja CPA Review.

Pricing: Ninja CPA Review has a unique payment structure in that you just pay a monthly fee to get access to the entire course. This is an advantage for folks on a budget who don't have a couple thousand dollars lying around.

Duct-Taped CPA Review Course: Unlike Roger CPA Review and Wiley CPAexcel, Ninja CPA Review lacks the cohesion that other CPA review courses have. They are quickly moving in the right direction but it's still three study resources duct-taped into one at this point (Jeff's Study Notes + Bisk Video Lectures (old and outdated) + the Ninja Test Bank).

TAKE ME TO NINJA CPA REVIEW

Lambers CPA Review
(Rank #9)

OVERALL RATING:

Experienced Lecturers: Lambers CPA Review is one of the oldest CPA review courses around and while not nearly as popular as other CPA courses makes a good option for CPA candidates who need audio lectures and a moneyback guarantee.

Course Format: The Lambers CPA exam materials are updated, however the course technology itself feels outdated and not very mobile friendly. They have very limited multiple choice analytics as well which makes it difficult to identify your weakenesses.

Pricing: A quick look at the Lambers website will show you how expensive the course is compared to other CPA review courses. They even charge you for customer service which seems to be a bit of a penny penching move to me.

TAKE ME TO LAMBERS CPA REVIEW
